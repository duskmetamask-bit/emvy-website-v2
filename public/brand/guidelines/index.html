01 · The brand p. 2

Calm expertise. Plain-spoken. Built to work.

EMVY is a B2B AI consultancy for growing businesses. We identify where AI fits in your operations, then build the systems to make it work. The brand should feel like a senior practitioner who’s done this a hundred times — quiet, considered, deeply competent. Not a salesperson. Not a hype cycle.

Positioning

The sentence a stranger reads first.

“We identify where your business can leverage AI, then build the systems to make it work.”

Audience

Who we’re talking to.

Owner-operators and small leadership teams at growing SMBs. Multi-vertical. They’re AI-curious, not AI-fluent. They need a guide, not a pitch.

What this brand is not

Not corporate, not breathless, not buzzwordy. Not “leveraging synergistic AI solutions.” Not “empowering the future of work.”

Not a tech-bro tone. Not aggressive. Not cute. We don’t try to be clever — we try to be useful.

02 · The mark · rules p. 4

Clear space & minimum size

The mark needs breathing room. Maintain clear space equal to ¼ of the mark’s height on all sides. Below 16 pixels the V cutout becomes hard to read — use the wordmark instead.

Clear space

Keep this much empty space around the mark on every side.

Minimum size

Use the SVG at any size. For PNG raster use, 64 px is the safe minimum; 32 px and 16 px renders are provided in the asset pack.

Do & don’t

Do
Use the mark on the brand cyan or on a flat dark surface.

Either on the page background or on a card with enough contrast.

Maintain the clear space — never crowd it.

No text, no imagery, no other marks inside the ¼-height buffer.

Don’t
Don’t recolour, gradient, or add effects.

No drop shadows, no glow, no bevel. The mark is a flat single colour.

Don’t stretch, rotate, or skew it.

Always render at the aspect ratio of the source viewBox.

03 · Color p. 5

One accent. A range of dark surfaces.

04 · Typography p. 6

Two sans, one mono. The same family across surfaces.

Space Grotesk for display and headings — a refined geometric sans with character. Manrope for body and UI — a humanist sans that’s quiet at small sizes. JetBrains Mono for code, data, and the rare typographic accent.

05 · Voice p. 7

Five rules. Read them as “if your sentence violates one, rewrite it.”

EMVY is a plain-spoken expert. We don’t do breathless, we don’t do jargon. We sound like a senior practitioner who’s done this a hundred times and is tired of hype.

Rule 01
Do

Direct, capable, human. Short sentences, no jargon.

Don’t

Corporate, breathless, hedge-y. Buzzwords. “Leverage.” “Empower.”

Rule 02
Do

Help first, pitch second. Diagnose before recommending.

Don’t

Lead with the sale. Open with credentials, awards, or jargon.

Rule 03
Do

Plain language for AI concepts. Name the tool, the workflow, the change.

Don’t

Gatekeep with “AI” as a black box. Hide the how behind buzzwords.

Rule 04
Do

Active verbs over adjectives. “Build” not “powerful.” “Find” not “innovative.”

Don’t

Stack adjectives. “Cutting-edge, transformative, world-class AI.”

Rule 05
Do

Specific over abstract. “Send me the next 30 days of invoices” not “optimise your finance workflow.”

Don’t

Vague aspiration. “Unlock the future of work.”
