# CRM V2 lifecycle and engagement contract — CRM-103/104

This additive contract preserves legacy records while introducing the V2
operational rules: active relationships need a human owner and exactly one
designated current task with a due date; nurture, lost, and closed records may
pause that requirement. Onboarding is blocked until approved scope evidence,
approver, and approval date are recorded. Engagements, approvals, and proof
cards remain human-owned records; nothing sends or publishes automatically.
