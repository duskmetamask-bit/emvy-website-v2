## Section 1: Your Time Audit

### Question 1: Repetitive Admin Tasks
**On average, how many hours per week does your team spend on repetitive admin tasks?**

These are tasks that follow the same process every time: data entry, scheduling, responding to routine enquiries, updating spreadsheets, following up with customers, generating standard reports.

- 0–2 hours/week: **1 point**
- 3–5 hours/week: **2 points**
- 6–10 hours/week: **3 points**
- 11–20 hours/week: **4 points**
- 20+ hours/week: **5 points**

---

### Question 2: Missed Calls and Enquiries
**How many incoming calls, emails, or messages go unanswered or unreplied-to each week?**

- None — we handle everything: **0 points**
- 1–5 per week: **1 point**
- 6–15 per week: **2 points**
- 16–30 per week: **3 points**
- 30+ per week: **4 points**

---

### Question 3: After-Hours Coverage
**Can your customers get answers to common questions outside of business hours?**

- Yes, we have staff or systems covering this: **0 points**
- No, but we have an answering service: **1 point**
- No, customers email/message and we respond next business day: **2 points**
- We lose enquiries regularly because of after-hours gaps: **3 points**

---

### Question 4: Customer Follow-Up
**When a new enquiry comes in, what happens?**

- Someone follows up immediately — every time: **0 points**
- We follow up within 24 hours most of the time: **1 point**
- We follow up when we remember or have time: **2 points**
- We have a backlog of unenquired leads we haven't contacted: **3 points**

## Section 2: Your Growth Constraints

### Question 5: Scaling Overhead
**When your revenue increases, what happens to your staff headcount?**

- Revenue grows without proportional headcount growth (systems handle the load): **0 points**
- We add one staff member for every significant revenue jump: **1 point**
- We're constrained — we can't take on more work without more people: **2 points**
- We've turned down work in the past 6 months because we don't have capacity: **3 points**

---

### Question 6: Consistency of Output
**When a task is done by different team members, is the result always the same quality and format?**

- Yes, we have documented processes and everyone follows them: **0 points**
- Mostly — some variation but it's acceptable: **1 point**
- No — quality and output varies significantly between people: **2 points**
- I can't delegate certain tasks because the result is never good enough: **3 points**

---

### Question 7: Business Intelligence
**How do you know what's happening in your business in real time?**

- We have dashboards or reports that update automatically: **0 points**
- We generate reports weekly or monthly manually: **1 point**
- We have a general sense but no clear data on most operations: **2 points**
- We're flying blind — we find out about problems when customers tell us: **3 points**

### Question 10: AI Implementation
**Has anyone in your business formally evaluated AI tools or automation for your operations?**

- Yes — we've implemented AI tools and they're working: **0 points**

- We've experimented with free AI tools casually but no formal assessment: **2 points**

**If you scored 0–8:**
